More Tictactoe

In an image on the takeaways page, you may have seen a weird looking board of tictactoe. It had a tictactoe board inside a tictactoe board, making it tictictactactoetoe, or tictactoeception. Either way, this can help introduce you to a bunch of variations of tictactoe. Here, I'll be listing a few:

  • Different Sized Board
    • 4x4
    • 2x3
    • 7x7
    • ...
  • More Players
    • 3 Player
    • 4 Player
    • 1 Player
    • 0 Player
    • ...
  • Alternate rules
    • 4 in a row wins
    • New symbols must be placed next to old symbols
    • Whoever makes more x in a row wins
    • You can place symbols over old symbols
    • Tictictactactoetoe/Tictactoeception
    • ...
